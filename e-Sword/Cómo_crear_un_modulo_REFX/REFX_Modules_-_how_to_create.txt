Yeah, basically what they said.

1. Create a .topx file.
2. Save it to your "documents/e-Sword" folder (NOT the "program files/e-Sword" folder).
3. Open e-Sword.
4. In the topic editor window, select your topx file.
5. Hold ctrl and right click.
6. Select "convert to reference book"
